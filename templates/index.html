Track Your Study Habits

Log your study hours and view your progress over time. Customize your goals and stay on track!




Features

  • Track study hours by subject

    Log and monitor the time spent studying each subject to better understand your focus areas. This feature allows you to record hours for different subjects and track your study habits over time.

  • View analytics and trends

    Analyze your study patterns with visual graphs and charts. Gain insights into how your study time is distributed across subjects and identify trends to optimize your study routine.

  • Earn achievements

    Unlock badges and achievements based on your study milestones and consistency. Celebrate your progress with awards for reaching study goals and maintaining productive study habits.
